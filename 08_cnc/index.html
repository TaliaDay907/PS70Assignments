<div class="textcontainer"> <p class="margin"> </p> <h3>Week 8: CNC Milling</h3> <h4>Assignment: Make Something With CNC</h4> <p class="margin"> For my CNC and Molding assignment, I decided to take a shot at making a Dalecarlian (or Dala) horse. Dala horses are a type of traditional carving originating from Sweden. I myself am not Swedish, but my childhood best friend is, and I remember seeing them everytime I went over to her house. This year she is graduating high school, so I thought it would be fitting for me to make a Dala horse as a graduation gift. Below on the left is a photo of a Dala horse with the traditional design. On the left is an outline I found online that I used as the base for my outline for my Fusion model. </p> <img width="500" src="../Dala_Horse.jpg" alt="week7-placeholder-image.jpg"> <img width="400" src="../DalaOutline.jpg" alt="week7-placeholder-image.jpg"> <p class="margin"> Credits: Bard Graduate Center: https://www.bgc.bard.edu/research/articles/90/dala-horse (left) Pattern Universe: https://patternuniverse.com/download/dala-horse-pattern/?utm_source=Pinterest&utm_medium=organic (right) </p> <p class="margin"> I started the 3D model of my horse with the intent of making the final shape/outline (bottom left).
